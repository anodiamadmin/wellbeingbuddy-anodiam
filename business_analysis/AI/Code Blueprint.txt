Safety Protocol During Baseline

Only trigger follow-up if:

“Very worried”

“Yes” on unsafe

3 consecutive low mood days

Follow-up must be:

One question only.

“Want to tell me what’s making it hard?”
Skip allowed.

Never probe deeply during baseline phase.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What You Collect Over 14 Days

You will now have:

14 Mood signals

~20–25 dimension signals

All 8 dimensions touched 2–3 times

Early baseline trends

Engagement habit established

Without overwhelming the child.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What Happens After Day 14?

Now you:

Establish baseline average per dimension

Identify consistently low dimensions

Begin adaptive rotation

Introduce slightly deeper optional questions

Adjust frequency per child
